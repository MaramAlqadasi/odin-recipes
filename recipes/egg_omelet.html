Ingregiants

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tomatos
  • 1 onion
  • 1 garlic
  • salt
  • hot paper

Steps

  1. Combine eggs, onion, hot pepper, tomatoes, and garlic in a bowl. Stir egg mixture well to combine.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium-low heat. Add oil. Pour egg mixture into the skillet.
  3. Cook until edges are dry, about 5 minutes.
  4. Flip omelet over.
  5. cook about 5 minutes more
